Frequently Asked Questions

To start investing, you need to create an account, complete your KYC verification, fund your wallet, and explore the available markets on our platform. Make sure to diversify your portfolio by investing in both crypto and stocks to minimize risk.

The minimum investment amount varies by asset class. For crypto, it starts as low as $10, while for stocks, it begins at $50. Check specific asset details on the platform for more accurate requirements.

We partner with regulated custodians and insurers to protect certain assets. Crypto assets may not be covered by traditional insurance policies, but stock investments are covered under SIPC insurance up to $500,000.

Withdrawals are easy. Go to your dashboard, click on 'Withdraw Funds,' choose your preferred payment method, and enter the withdrawal amount. Processing times may vary based on your bank or payment provider.
